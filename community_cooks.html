Fudgy Brownies

Ingredients

150g butter, 125g Plain Flour, 200g Caster sugar, 2 Large eggs, 2 tsp vanilla extract, 100g chocolate bar (chopped), 4 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tsp salt

Instructions

Chocolate and Cappuccino Cake

Ingredients

For the Cake:
200g Soft butter, 175g Caster sugar, 140g Self-raising flour, 4 large eggs, 2 tsp Baking powder, 5 tbsp cocoa powder

For the Filling: 150g Icing sugar, 1 tsp coffee powder, 100 Butter

Instructions

Cream the butter and sugar together, until fluffy. Add the whisked eggs and keep mixing. Add the rest of the ingredients, and beat gently. Pour mixture into separate tins to be layered together. Cook at 180 degrees for 30-45 minutes. Meanwhile, whisk all ingredients for the filling together, until light and fluffy. Decorate as you please!

Chocolate Clusters

Ingredients

50g butter, 100g Cornflakes, 100g Chocolate (Milk or Dark), 2 tbsp Golden Syrup

Instructions

Put the butter, chocolate and golden syrup into a saucepan, and the cornflakes in a separate bowl. Melt ingredients that are in the saucepan over low heat, cool a little before putting on cornflakes. Stir everything together, and put in cupcake cases; add toppings if wanted. Then put in the fridge to cool before serving.
