About our project

Our vision for this project is to have a platform that empowers people, embeds content and celebrates positivity in varied forms; from artwork, to cooking recipes and poems. Submission options are endless. Be part of our community!

Meet the team

We are a group of ladies who met on the Code First Girls Introduction to Web Development course, practicing HTML, CSS & Javascript through making this platform. COVID-19 meant everything was online and we managed to make this website without meeting each other in person!

Helen

Helen is currently working as a Team Leader at a materials testing company called Intertek, based in Derby. She grew up in US, Taiwan and UK, having moved to London at the age of 10. She graduated from Imperial College London with a degree in Materials Science and Engineering, and is studying a part-time Master's at University of Surrey.

Juggling between work and going back into education proved to be a bit of a challenge. Outside of work, she is a keen traveller and loves meeting people from different background and is always on the lookout for new experiences.

She enrolled on the CFG Intro to Web Development to develop her coding skills further, after an unsuccessful attempt at university.

Mimp

Mimp is a Designer with interests in social design, innovation, and change. Her practice lies in mixed media, design thinking and research, conceptual development and now web development. Having a multi-disciplinary background, she is an adaptable team player with transferable skills. She is currently open for opportunities.
